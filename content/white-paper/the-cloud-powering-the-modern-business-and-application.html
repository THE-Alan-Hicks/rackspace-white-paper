The cloud is one of the largest shifts in IT over the past decade, fueling innovation across the globe. Today, it powers many businesses and mission-critical applications, allowing organizations to take advantage of its inherent flexibility. However, many companies have yet to adopt the cloud as part of their IT strategy. Rackspace has put together a slide deck to help people pitch the merits of cloud computing inside their company. This deck covers the following topics:

  • Statistics of cloud adoption
  • Five cloud advantages
  • Different workloads suited for the cloud
  • Raw cloud infrastructure versus a managed cloud
  • Advantages of a managed cloud
  • Next steps to get to the cloud
