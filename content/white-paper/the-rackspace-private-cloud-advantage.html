Rackspace Private Cloud is powered by OpenStack® and delivers the agility and efficiency of a public cloud combined with the enhanced security, control and performance of a dedicated environment. It can be deployed in your data center or ours, is managed by our OpenStack experts and backed by Fanatical Support®. Rackspace Private Cloud gives you all the power of the cloud without the pain of running it, so you can focus on your core business.

  • Scalability with Availability
    Rackspace Private Cloud is built to power your enterprise production workloads. It includes an industry-leading 99.99% OpenStack API uptime guarantee and is designed to scale to hundreds of nodes.
     
  • Application-level Automation
    Use automation to unlock the full power and value of your cloud. With DevOps Automation Services1 we help automate your application environment by treating your infrastructure as code.
     
  • Expertise
    Who better to build and manage your private cloud than the company who co-founded OpenStack and now runs one of the world’s largest OpenStack-powered clouds.
     
  • Fanatical Support
    Our exceptional customer service means we’re in it with you every step of the way. And it includes teams of specialists who proactively monitor and maintain the health of your private cloud and are available to help you 24x7x365.
     
  • Hybrid
    Our hybrid portfolio enables you to burst into our public cloud and helps you get the right fit for each of your applications by using RackConnect® to securely connect your Rackspace Private Cloud with the Rackspace Public Cloud or our dedicated hosting platform.
     
  • Deploy Anywhere
    We offer you the flexibility to deploy Rackspace Private Cloud in your data center, our data center or in both. And no matter where it is deployed, our team of experts will manage your private cloud, so you don’t have to.

Rackspace Private Cloud provides a complete managed private cloud solution–powered by OpenStack, designed to support enterprise production workloads, managed by our OpenStack experts, and backed by our award-winning Fanatical Support.

1 Only available for environments hosted at Rackspace.
